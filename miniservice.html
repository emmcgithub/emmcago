Emmcago Agro Farm is a proudly indigenous agricultural business dedicated to helping farmers grow high-quality oil palm and coconut plantations. Our goal is simple: to fight the spread of fake and low-yield seedlings by providing trusted, affordable, and high-performing varieties.
